Journal Features

Daily/Weekly Reflections

Structured prompts guide your daily and weekly reflections on lessons, student engagement, and classroom management.

Lesson Planning

Comprehensive templates for lesson planning with space for objectives, materials, differentiation strategies, and assessments.

Feedback Integration

Dedicated sections for mentor feedback with action plans to implement suggestions and track progress.

Professional Growth

Track your professional development activities, workshops, and how you implement new strategies in your classroom.

Portfolio Artifacts

Upload and organize student work, lesson plans, and other evidence of your teaching practice and growth.

Evidence-Based

Prompts encourage you to base reflections on specific observations and evidence from your classroom.
